## What is Login Streak?

**Login Streak** is a daily reward system that tracks your consecutive logins. Each day you log in, you unlock a new reward to claim. 

## Where can I find Login Streak?

You can access Login Streak from the main menu. Look for the streak indicator showing your current consecutive days.

## How does the reward cycle work?

The reward system runs on a **30-day cycle**. Each day you log in unlocks a new reward slot. After completing all 30 days, the cycle resets and you can earn the rewards again.

## What are Milestone Days?

**Milestone Days** offers enhanced rewards. These special days include bonus items and higher currency amounts compared to regular days.

## Do I have to claim rewards in order?

No! You can claim any unlocked reward in any order. If you forget to claim a reward on day 3 but log in on day 5, you can still go back and claim day 3's reward.

## What happens if I miss a day?

If you miss logging in for a day, your streak resets to 1 and any unclaimed rewards are lost. Make sure to log in daily to maintain your streak and maximize your rewards.

## Can I claim multiple days at once?

Yes. If you've logged in multiple days but haven't claimed rewards, you can claim all your unlocked days whenever you want. Just tap on each day to collect. but do not miss any days otherwise your streak will reset and unclaimed rewards will be lost.

## Any tips for Login Streak?

- **Log in daily**: Even if you can't play, a quick login keeps your streak alive.
- **Prioritize milestones**: The bonus rewards on days 7, 14, 21, and 30 are worth the effort.
- **Claim before reset**: Don't forget to claim rewards before your streak breaks.
