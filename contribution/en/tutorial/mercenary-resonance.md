## What is Neural Resonance?

**Neural Resonance** is an advanced technique that allows you to strengthen your 5-star mercenaries by synchronizing their neural patterns with another compatible mercenary. Through this process, dormant potential is awakened within your target mercenary, making them significantly more powerful. The ultimate goal is ascending your mercenary to the **Mythic** tier.

## Where can I find Neural Resonance?

You can access Neural Resonance from the **Red Bar**. Look for the option to speak with **Siren**, who holds court in the VIP booth. She's the one who performs the resonance procedure.

## How does Neural Resonance work?

The process involves two mercenaries:
- **Target**: The mercenary you want to strengthen (this one stays and gets more powerful)
- **Catalyst**: The mercenary you sacrifice (their combat experience transfers to the target)

When you perform resonance, the catalyst's neural patterns merge with the target's, awakening hidden potential. The catalyst mercenary is consumed in the process, but you receive some resources back if you have invested resources in them before.

## What mercenaries can participate in Resonance?

Only **5-star** mercenaries can participate in Neural Resonance. Both the target and catalyst must be 5-star, and they must share the **exact same ability**. For example, a mercenary with the Single Target Heal ability can only resonate with another mercenary that also has the Single Target Heal ability - not one with Group Heal.

Mythic mercenaries cannot participate since they've already reached maximum potential.

## What is Awakening Level?

Each successful resonance increases your target mercenary's **Awakening Level**. The progression goes:
- 5-star → **+1** → **+2** → **+3** → **+4** → **Mythic**

Each awakening level provides a power boost. When you reach +4 and perform one more resonance, your mercenary ascends to Mythic.

## How do I perform Neural Resonance?

1. Visit **Siren's Booth** at the Red Bar
2. Tap `Begin Neural Resonance`
3. Select your **Target** mercenary (the one you want to strengthen)
4. Select your **Catalyst** mercenary (must have the same ability as your target)
5. Review the preview showing your mercenary's new power level
6. Tap `INITIATE RESONANCE` to confirm
7. Watch the synchronization process complete

## How many mercenaries do I need to reach Mythic?

You need a total of **6 five-star mercenaries** with the same ability to create one Mythic:
- 1 base mercenary (your target)
- 5 catalysts (sacrificed one at a time through 5 resonance procedures)

## What happens to the catalyst mercenary?

The catalyst mercenary is consumed during resonance. However, you receive **partial resources back** from your investment:
- A portion of the **Cybernetic Enhancers** used to level them
- A portion of the **Neural Upgrade Augmenters** used to upgrade them

This means investing in a mercenary is never completely wasted, even if you later use them as a catalyst.

## How much stronger does my mercenary become?

Each awakening level increases your mercenary's power. You can see the exact power increase in the preview before confirming any resonance. The power boost affects all stats and ability effectiveness.

## What if I don't have matching mercenaries?

If you have a 5-star mercenary but no other 5-stars with the same ability, you won't be able to resonate that mercenary yet. You'll need to recruit more mercenaries and hope for duplicates with matching abilities. The rarer the ability, the harder it may be to find matches.

## Can I reverse a resonance?

No, resonance is permanent. Once you sacrifice a catalyst, that mercenary is gone forever. Make sure you're certain before confirming the procedure. The preview screen shows you exactly what will happen before you commit.
